Ancient Rome

A visual journey through the Eternal City's iconic monuments

The Colosseum

The largest ancient amphitheater ever built, the Colosseum once held 50,000 spectators for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

The Pantheon

Originally a temple to all Roman gods, the Pantheon features the world's largest unreinforced concrete dome, with its famous oculus open to the sky.

The Roman Forum

Once the center of Roman public life, the Forum contains the ruins of ancient government buildings, temples, and triumphal arches spanning centuries of history.
