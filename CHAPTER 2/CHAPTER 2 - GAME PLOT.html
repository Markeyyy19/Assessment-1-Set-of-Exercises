World of Warcraft: Midnight

The Worldsoul Saga — Part II

Official Website

Plot

Midnight picks up after The War Within. The heroes allied with Xal'atath to stop a world-ending threat — but it was a trap. She now leads a full Void invasion against Quel'Thalas, home of the Blood Elves.

A massive Voidstorm threatens to destroy the Sunwell, the sacred source of light and magic. If it falls, the world is plunged into eternal darkness.

Players must reunite the fractured elven peoples and lead the defence, ending with a climactic march on the Sunwell Plateau.
